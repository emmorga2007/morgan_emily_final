The Toilet Cleaner

A new take on our traditional recipe. With a robust depth of flavour, this pale ale is sure to please. Enjoy this strong beer, intended for the experienced consumer and aptly named for - if you aren’t careful - you’ll be cleaning the toilet in the morning!

The Daily Cleanser

Inspired by Lysol's daily cleanser products. This beer is a mix of Kombucha and SBC's traditional brew. The sourness of the kombucha mixes delightfully with the sweet taste of jasmine and malt of our beer. Enjoy the combination of effervescence and flavours in this probiotic rich beer.

All-Purpose Patio Beer

This drink owes its name to Lysol's all-purpose line of cleaning products, inspired by this duality, we have created a beverage to be enjoyed at any patio. Weather it's a backyard BBQ, late night firesides, or just a casual get togehter.Enjoy this hoppy pale ale, with fnishing crisp notes of roasted malt.

The Germ Killer

A smooth and inviting flavour that morphs over time to a gentle lime-citrus note. Enjoy this low calorie bubbly beverage thats the vitamin enriched answer to guilt free beer. Bound to please any craft beer lover's taste buds.
