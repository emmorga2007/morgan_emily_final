The Toilet Cleaner

The Toilet Cleaner. A strong beer intended for the experienced consumer. Enjoy our new take on the traditional Strathroy brewing recipe in this aptly named drink for if you aren’t careful, you’ll be cleaning the toilet tomorrow morning!

The Daily Cleanser

Inspired by Lysol's daily cleanser products.This beer is a mix of Kombucha and SBC's traditional brew.The sourness of the kombucha mixes delightfully with the sweetness of our beer. Enjoy the combination of effervesence and flavours in this probiotic rich beer.

All-Purpose Patio Beer

The All-Purpose Patio Beer. A strong amber ale fit for any patio BBQ’s.

The Germ Killer

The Germ Killer. The vitamin enriched answer to guilt free bree. Enjoy this low calorie bubbly beer infusied with the tart taste if citrus. Best enjoyed cold!
